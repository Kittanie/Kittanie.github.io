About Me

Kittanie is my original gamer tag from when I was a child, it has now become my branding for this business. The graphic design aspect started at my old technical production job and followed me into my twitch channel. I started out doing small touch ups to existing project's and over time started teaching myself different techniques & software. Although, I have done smaller jobs involving programming and website code, the design aspect is the most fulfilling for me. I am not wanting to just hand over an export file and be done, I want to build a relationship with my clients. With me, you're not just getting a design, you're getting fully committed investment into your business or online profile. I care about how you progress and without you, I wouldn't be here.

Twitch

I started out on Twitch.tv in January 2017, quite late into the streaming game. The channel started out playing CS:GO with friends without me having any idea exactly what this platform could do. With the help of the twitch community, I have made new friends, started a small graphic design business and have made the kitten army, which is the channels community that makes gaming more social and interactive. Although my streaming schedule is offline for the moment, in the future I will put more focus into the channel.
